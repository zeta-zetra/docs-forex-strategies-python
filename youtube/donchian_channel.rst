Donchian Channel  
=================

This is the famous Donchian channel. Here is the `source <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ncJKep-6vU8>`_ of strategy.


Buy Rules 
---------

1. Close is greater than the upper channel.




Sell Rules
----------

1. Close is less than the lower channel.
